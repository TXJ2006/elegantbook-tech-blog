\section{Optimization}

A standard regularized empirical risk objective can be written as
\[
  \min_{\theta} \frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}\ell(f_{\theta}(x_i), y_i)
  + \lambda\|\theta\|_2^2.
\]
